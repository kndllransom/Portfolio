About Me

Hey, I'm Kendall, a Software Developer with a passion for photography and nature. It's actually funny how I got here, I'll explain. I was born and raised Brooklyn, NY, and lived the remainder of my teenage years in New Jersey. As a son of two law enforcement officers, as well as a good amount of my family being military, I thought it was my path... or something like that. Anyway! When I turned 20 I decided to enlist in the U.S. Air Force as a Military Police officer. I spent the next 6 years stationed at Patrick Space Force Base (Formerly known as Patrick Air Force Base), only a couple miles south of Cocoa Beach, Florida! I separated from the military in May of 2020 with an application in hand for the Houston Police Department. As I was about to send in my application, I realized that police work wasn't my passion, but creativity was. So I picked up a fairly new hobby, photography(I mean I've used my phone for selfies and stuff, sure). But I decided to go out and get a real camera and I've been hooked ever since! After a while I started looking for another creative outlet, something out of my comfort zone. I was talking to one of my sisters about it one day and she had reminded me about how I used to design layouts for Myspace... LOL don't judge me, you probably had one too. After thinking about it for a while and playing with coding simulators for a few months, I wanted to learn how to actually use this skill! So I went to school for software engineering. I will say that it definitely was a learning curve at first, but with help, I learned to see the creative aspect of coding which I now love!
